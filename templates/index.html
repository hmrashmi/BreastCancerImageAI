📚 Medical Classification Terms

🟢 Benign

Benign tissue is NON-CANCEROUS. It is normal, healthy breast tissue that poses no cancer risk. The cells grow normally and do not spread to other parts of the body.

🔴 Malignant

Malignant tissue is CANCEROUS. These abnormal cells multiply uncontrollably and can infiltrate nearby tissue or spread throughout the body. This requires immediate medical consultation.
